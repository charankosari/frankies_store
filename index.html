About Us

Welcome to OG Frankies! At OG Frankies, we bring the vibrant flavors of Indian street food to your table with a modern twist. Our journey began with a passion for authentic, mouthwatering cuisine and a commitment to quality. Each Frankie is crafted using homemade bread and the freshest ingredients, ensuring every bite is packed with unforgettable taste. Our menu features a range of delicious Frankies, from spicy Chilli Paneer to creamy Butter Chicken, all made with traditional spices and our special blend of masala. We take pride in our in-house preparation, free from preservatives, to deliver a genuine and wholesome culinary experience. Whether you're a longtime fan of Indian street food or trying it for the first time, we invite you to join us at OG Frankies and savor the exceptional flavors that make our food truly special. Thank you for being a part of our journey – we can't wait to share our passion for great food with you!
